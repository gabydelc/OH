Dishwashing

Dishwashing is probably subject to more variables than any other household clean-up job. In theory at least, any detergent or soap can be used for hand dishwashing, except an automatic dishwasher detergent. However, soap has become almost extinct as a dishwashing product and many laundry detergents are not very acceptable. Laundry detergents may leave spots and streaks, the powders may not completely dissolve, or may discolor certain metals with soaking. A light-duty liquid hand dishwashing detergent is the best choice for hand washing.
