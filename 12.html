Comfort

From Eames to Gehry, countless design visionaries have left their mark on the American rocking chair, creating an object that synthesizes both napping and modernism.
The first rocking chair was invented in 1710 by tying two ice skates to the bottom of a chair, but it wasn't till 1787 that "rocking chair" finally entered the Oxford English Dictionary for Usage. At this time, rocking chairs were often designed for two or more people: a mother and child, an elderly person and his or her nurse, Beyonce and Jay, etc.
